Privacy

Privacy Policy

We design our products to respect your time, attention, and data. This policy explains what information is collected, how it’s used, and the choices you have.

Effective date: January 1, 2026
Applies to: MySightWordz

Overview

MySightWordz is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe children’s learning apps should be safe, simple, and free from data collection practices that put families at risk.

This Privacy Policy explains how MySightWordz handles information when you use the app.

MySightWordz does not collect any personal data.

Specifically

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not collect usage analytics
  • We do not track users across apps or websites
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies

All app usage stays on the device.

Children's Privacy

MySightWordz is intended for use by children with parental guidance.

We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by:

  • Collecting no personal information
  • Using no third-party tracking or analytics
  • Displaying no advertising

Becuase no data is collected, there is no data to share, sell, or store.

Advertising and Tracking

MySightWordz

  • Contains no ads
  • Uses no third-party SDKs
  • Includes no tracking of any kind
  • Has no in-app purchases or subscriptions

Your child will never be marketed to inside the app.

Data Storage

Any custom sight word lists or progress information you create are stored locally on your device only.

We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to any server.

Third-party Services

MySightWordz does not integratre with:

  • Social media platforms
  • Advertising networks
  • Analytics providers
  • Any other third-party services that collect data

There are no third-party services involved in data handling.

Your Choices and Control

Becuase MySightWordz does not collect data:

  • There is nothing to opt out of
  • Nothing to request deletion of
  • Nothing stored externally

Deleting the app removes all locally stored information from your device.

Changes to this policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.

Any future changes will continue to prioritize privacy, simplicity, and safety for children and families.
